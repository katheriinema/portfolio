Tech Stack 🚀

  • Back End
    • Python & Flask: Built the majority of our backend with Flask, managing routes & APIs.
    • LangChain & Groq API: Powered our conversational AI chatbot with advanced NLP.
    • Replit: Used for collaborative coding and backend prototyping.
  • Front End
    • React.js: Dynamic, responsive user interface.
    • Custom CSS: Unique styles aligned with TravelHER’s branding.

Description

Built at Purdue’s 2025 InnovateHer hackathon in 36 hours, TravelHER helps (female) solo travelers craft safe, custom itineraries. My team and I designed a React‐based frontend, leveraged several Google APIs for dynamic route planning and location tracking, and integrated ChatGPT to provide real-time safety tips.
